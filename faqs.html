Registering your team

If you are looking to register for one of our leagues, it's a really quick and simple process. Just follow the steps below that tells you everything you need to know:

  • To register a team, search for your nearest league
  • Click and view the league you wish to join
  • Click "JOIN NOW" to enter a team into the league
  • Fill out your information then click "JOIN NOW"
  • Edit your team information, enter your team name, add your personal information as the team captain, upload a player card photo and fill out your contact information.
  • Click "ENTER TEAM" to enter
  • You will be taken to the payment page
  • After payment, you will be taken to your team profile afterwards
